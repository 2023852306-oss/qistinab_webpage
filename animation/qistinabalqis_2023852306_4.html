A wall in a building is a vertical structure that encloses, divides, or supports a space. Walls can be structural, meaning they are load-bearing and support the weight of the floors and roof, or non-load-bearing, serving as partitions to divide rooms. They are built from various materials like brick, concrete, wood, or metal, and provide functions such as shelter, security, thermal insulation, and soundproofing.
